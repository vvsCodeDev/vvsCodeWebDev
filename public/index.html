About

vvsCode is a Mobile App development firm focused on delivering high-impact robust Android & iOS apps. Our apps use a Google Cloud Platform backend for customer data and authentication. We use the Scrum Agile development methodology along with Kanban to promote a sense of clarity among the developers that promotes job satisfaction and helps avoid costly churn. Utilizing the tools of product ownership such as Jira and Confluence, vvsCode is able to get everyone onboard with a single vision and move forward methodically to deploy on time and under budget
